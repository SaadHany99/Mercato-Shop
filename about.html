Mercato is a huge commercial market that was established in 1999 by its owner, Engineer Saad Hani. It was initially a huge market with branches throughout the cities and villages of Egypt, then it was decided to make it available online by the owners in 2023, and the site was launched in January 2024. The application includes several different products, such as:

Some famous Egyptian and Italian meals

Some luxurious drinks and juices

Elegant clothes

You can log in through the application, browse various products, add what you need to buy to the shopping cart, and order it to be delivered to you without fatigue or hassle. The owners of Mercato aim for the application to become the most famous in the world of commercial markets at the level of the Arab and African countries and to expand globally as soon as possible. All this success is thanks to the good reputation that Mercato has left with its customers and their satisfaction with its products
